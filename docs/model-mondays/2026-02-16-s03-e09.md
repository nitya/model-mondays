### Tech Spotlight: Foundry Labs

Want to experiment with cutting edge models and advanced tools from Microsoft Research? Foundry Labs provides access to experimental projects and innovative technologies before they reach general availability.

**Key Features:**
- Early access to experimental models
- Cutting-edge tools and frameworks
- Integration with Microsoft Research projects
- Community feedback and collaboration
- Rapid prototyping capabilities

**Featured Projects:**
- **Magentic-UI**: AI-powered UI generation
- **Fara**: Advanced agentic framework
- **Phi-4**: Powerful small language model
- Additional research innovations
